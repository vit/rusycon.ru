Chaos Control Bibliography (1997 - 2000)


A.L.Fradkov

Institute for Problems of Mechanical Engineering
St.Petersburg, RUSSIA



This bibliography contains about 700 references to the papers published in
peer reviewed journals in 1997-2000, as well as the list of related books,
surveys, special issues of journals and conference proceedings published
since 1994. It was prepared using Science Citation Index
(http://www.webofscience.com/) when the author was staying in the University of Melbourne
in Oct-Dec, 2000.

When using this bibliography as a reference, please cite as:
A.L.Fradkov, Chaos Control Bibliography (1997 - 2000).
Russian Systems and Control Archive (RUSYCON), www.rusycon.ru/chaos-control.html

A useful source of earlier references is the bibliography
"Control and Synchronization of Chaotic Systems", created by G.Chen:
www.ee.cityu.edu.hk/~gchen/chaos-bio.html
(see also: Chen, G., and X. Dong, (1998), From chaos to order: perspectives,
methodologies and applications, World Scientific, Singapore.)

Comments/additions can be e-mailed to fradkov@mail.ru or info@rusycon.ru
