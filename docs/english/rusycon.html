Russian Systems and Control Archive (RUSYCON)


Welcome to RUSYCON - Russian Systems and Control Archive! RUSYCON contains unique information about Russian specialists, groups, institutions and companies, foundations, conferences, journals, books - everything related to systems and control. More than 2000 references to useful sources in Russia and worldwide are collected, including links to general references and databases in systems and control, information and educational sites, virtual laboratories and online experiments, sites on nonlinear dynamics, complexity and chaos, etc. Electronic journal RUSYCON is aimed at express publishing new and discussion materials. RUSYCON is designed in order to facilitate and enhance cooperation between experts in the field of systems and control in Russia and worldwide. Information is updated on a regular basis and presented both in English and in Russian.
All your comments/additions/suggestions are welcome.
