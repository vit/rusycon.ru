The annual meeting of the Saint-Petersburg Group of Russian National Committee on Automatic Control took place on February 18, 2009 in the CSRI "Elektropribor". The events of the year 2008 were discussed and the annual report of 2008 was approved. The calendar of the conferences of the year 2009 and the list of the seminars of the year 2009 were approved.
