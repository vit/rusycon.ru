EECI International Graduate School on Control 2014 (EECI-IGSC-2014)
 EECI International Graduate School on Control 2014 (EECI-IGSC-2014) will take place in spring, 2014 at different locations worldwide, see http://eeci-institute.eu/IGSC2014. Of special interest is the lecture course NONLINEAR CONTROL FOR PHYSICAL SYSTEMS to take place in St. Petersburg, Russia on April 14-19, 2014. The lecturers are Roger W. Brockett, Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, USA and Alexander L. Fradkov, RAS, Saint Petersburg, Russia.

Attendees of the lecture course will have a possibility to participate in the conference CONTROL OF PHYSICAL SYSTEMS with their poster presentations. Selected papers presented at the conference will be published in a special issue of the IPACS journal CYBERNETICS AND PHYSICS. Deadline for advance registration is Dec.20, 2013. Travel, accomodation and social events info will be available soon at ww.eeci-institute.eu/IGSC2014.
