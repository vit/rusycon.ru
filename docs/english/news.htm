News

On February 11, 2021, the annual meeting of the St. Petersburg group of the Russian National Committee for Automatic Control (SPb group RNKAU) was held at the Concern "Central Research Institute "Electropribor". The meeting was held in a hybrid (face-to-face and distance) format. The results of work in 2020 were discussed and the work plan for 2021 was approved. The list of conferences and seminars to be held in 2021 has been approved.
