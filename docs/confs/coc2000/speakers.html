COC 2000 - Information for Speakers

English will be the official language. No simultaneous translation will be provided. Overhead projectors will be available.

Alloted time for oral presentations:

    Plenary talk 45 minutes
    Semi-Plenary talk 40 minutes
    Regular and invited presentation 20 minutes
    (including 5 minutes for discussion)

Posters will be presented on July 5 from 17:30 till 18:30 in the foyer of the White Hall. Vertically oriented space 120cm by 80cm will be provided for each paper.
